build Installation

Step-by-step installation process of the Insylo system.

warning TAKE THE FOLLOWING MEASURES

Before climbing up and down the silo

Take all the measurements you can from the ground, this way you avoid having to go back down once you're up there. Also make sure to take all the necessary measurements from above so you don't have to go up again.

01 qr_code

Locate the QR Sticker

Locate the Insylo QR code sticker on the silo leg (accessible and protected from damage).

02 photo_camera

Take Photos of the Silo

  • check_circle The silo label(s) with specifications
  • check_circle The Insylo QR code with the silo label
03 edit

Mark the Position

Access the top of the silo. In an area where the silo is not fed, make a mark approximately 40 cm from the lower edge of the top cone.

04 center_focus_strong

Align the Adapter

Make sure to align the top of the silo adapter with the center of the silo.

05 crop_square

Mark the Rectangle

Mark the inner rectangle of the silo adapter.

warning WARNING: Do not mark the mounting holes in this step.
06 content_cut

Cut the Rectangle

Cut the marked rectangle on the silo. Be careful not to let the cut piece fall inside.

07 handyman

Place the Adapter

Using the silo adapter again, place it over the hole making sure there is no silo surface protruding from the lower inner part of the adapter. It is very important to position the base as shown in the image.

08 construction

Drill the Holes

Mark and drill the mounting holes.

09 build

Install the Adapter

Install the fixing adapter with M6x30 screws and M6 self-locking nuts included.

10 settings

Adjust the Screws

Do not overtighten the silo adapter screws (the gasket should compress approximately 50%).

warning If the base deforms due to screw pressure, water leaks may occur.
11 format_color_fill

Seal the Perimeter

Seal the perimeter of the silo adapter with silicone (suitable for outdoor use).

12 straighten

Measure the Silo Angle

Measure the angle of the silo top cone. You can use an inclinometer, protractor or angle measuring device.

13 unfold_more

Loosen the Nut

Loosen the nut to adjust the camera angle.

14 tune

Adjust the Camera Angle

  • check_circle Each mark on the camera equals 10º (1 = 10º)
  • check_circle Take the silo angle measurement and subtract 10º
  • check_circle Adjust the device to the resulting angle

Example: If the silo angle is 50º, then 50º - 10º = 40º = position 4. The device should be set to position 4.

15 lock

Fix the Angle

Tighten the nut to permanently fix the camera angle.

16 power

Turn on the Device

Turn on the Insylo device using the power switch located at the bottom of the device.

17 upload

Place the Device

Place the Insylo device on the silo adapter.

18 check_circle

Finish Installation

Tighten the screws.

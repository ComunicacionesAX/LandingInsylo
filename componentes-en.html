checklist Preparation

Read the viability requirements and risks that must be met before starting the installation work.

accessibility Accessibility

The area around the silo must be accessible to place the necessary equipment to access the top of the silo. Also, the location where the sensor will be installed should not be where the silo is loaded to avoid damage to the sensor.

warning High Voltage Lines

The top of the silo must be free of high voltage lines or other structures that may affect the installation.

security PPE and Work at Heights

Personal protection equipment and equipment for work at heights should be used.

wb_sunny Sunlight

Confirm that the device can be installed in a location oriented towards the sun.

explore Seasonal Zones

Orientation may vary depending on the region of the world. The device orientation should be towards the south, this is essential to ensure proper operation.
